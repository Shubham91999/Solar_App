About the Project



At Solar Energy Prediction, we are dedicated to harnessing the power of predictive analytics to help advance the use of solar energy. As the world moves towards sustainable energy solutions, solar energy has emerged as a key player in reducing carbon footprints and meeting global energy demands.

Solar energy production is influenced by a variety of factors such as solar irradiance, temperature, wind speed, and geographical location. Understanding how these factors interact can significantly enhance our ability to predict solar energy outputs, making it possible to optimize energy generation and storage systems.

Our project focuses on the state of Arizona, a region with abundant sunlight and a high potential for solar energy generation. By gathering and analyzing historical solar energy data, we have developed a predictive model that estimates solar energy production based on real-time environmental factors.

The goal of this project is to empower stakeholders—including homeowners, businesses, and energy planners—by providing accurate solar energy predictions. This will enable better decision-making in terms of energy usage, sustainability, and cost-efficiency.
